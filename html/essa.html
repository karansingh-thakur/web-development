Electronics Students And Staff Association

Who We Are

Electronics Students and Staff Association or ESSA is a committee of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Students which aims to play a role in overall development of students in technical aspects.

What We Do

We are the team of Electronics students which works for sharpening the skills of the students, improving their abilities as well as their technical knowledge by arranging different Events, Seminars and Workshops.
